Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our facility management services.

What geographic areas do you serve?
We primarily serve properties across Kolar and Bangalore (including apartments, corporate parks, and industrial corridors). Contact our desk to verify support for your specific street or layout.
Are your staff members background-verified?
Yes, absolutely. Integrity and security are our top priorities. Every guard, housekeeper, and repair technician undergoes criminal background verification, address checks, and training before deployment.
Do you provide customized plans for smaller offices?
Yes, we do. We understand that a startup or small office has different operational budgets and hours compared to large tech parks. We offer flexible schedules—like alternate day housekeeping—to match your exact scale.
How do you handle absenteeism or emergency replacements?
Our operations office maintains active standby staff in key hubs. If an assigned guard or janitor is absent, a background-verified standby worker is deployed to your premises immediately to prevent service disruptions.
